About A.P.J Abdul Kalam

A.P.J the "Missile Man of India" was born on October 15 in 1931 in Rameswaram at TamilNadu he
served as the 11th President of India and was a renowned scientist known for his work in india's space and missile programs

Achievements

  • Developed India's first satellite launch vehicle(SLV-III)

  • Led India's successful nuclear tests in 1998

  • Qrote books like "Wings of Fire and "India 2020"

  • Received the Bharat Ratna,India's highest civilian award
